## Template Sharing

With a paid subscription, you can publish your card templates to the community browser so other users can use them.

### Publishing a template

1. Open the template you want to share in the Card Designer.
2. Open the sharing settings from the template menu.
3. Click **Publish** to make the template available in the community browser.

Published templates are visible to all users. They can subscribe to your template and use it with their own data.

### Subscribing to templates

Browse published templates in the community browser. When you subscribe:

- The template is downloaded to your local storage.
- You receive updates when the publisher releases a new version.
- You can unsubscribe at any time without losing your local copy.

### Template limits

The number of templates you can create and publish depends on your subscription tier:

| Tier | Templates |
|------|-----------|
| Free | 2 |
| Premium | 4 |
| Creator | 10 |
