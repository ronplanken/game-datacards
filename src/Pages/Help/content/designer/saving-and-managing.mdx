## Saving and Managing Templates

Your work saves automatically.

Every change you make is saved to your browser within about a second. You'll see a status indicator showing whether your template is saved, saving, or has unsaved changes.

### Export and import

- <Icon name="Download" /> **Export** a template as a JSON file to back it up or share it with others
- <Icon name="Upload" /> **Import** a JSON file to load a template someone else created

### Duplicate

<Icon name="Copy" /> Create a copy of any template to experiment without affecting the original. Duplicates are named with "(Copy)" appended.

### Important

<Callout type="warning">
Templates are stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing your browser data will remove them. Use export to keep backups of templates you want to keep.
</Callout>
