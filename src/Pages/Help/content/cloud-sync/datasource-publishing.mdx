## Datasource Publishing

With a paid subscription, you can publish your custom datasources to the <Icon name="Globe" /> community browser so other users can subscribe to them.

### Publishing workflow

1. **Create** your datasource locally using the Datasource Editor.
2. **Enable sync** using the <Icon name="RefreshCw" /> toggle to upload it to your cloud account.
3. <Icon name="Share2" /> **Publish** the datasource from the sharing settings. This makes it visible in the community browser.

Published datasources have two versions:

- <IconLabel icon="Pencil" label="Edit version" /> — Your working copy. Changes here are private until you publish again.
- <IconLabel icon="Globe" label="Published version" /> — What subscribers see. Updated only when you explicitly publish.

### Subscribing to datasources

Browse published datasources in the community browser. When you subscribe:

- The datasource schema is downloaded to your local storage.
- You receive updates when the publisher releases a new version.
- You can unsubscribe at any time without losing your local copy.

### Limits

The number of datasources you can publish depends on your subscription tier. See the plans and pricing page for current limits.
