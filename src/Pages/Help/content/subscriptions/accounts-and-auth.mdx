## Accounts and Authentication

### Creating an account

Sign up with your email address from the login page. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Once verified, you can log in and access premium features based on your subscription tier.

### Two-factor authentication

For additional security, you can enable two-factor authentication (2FA) from your account settings. When enabled, you will need to enter a verification code from your authenticator app each time you log in.

### Session management

Your login session persists across browser refreshes. If you log in on multiple devices, each device maintains its own session. Logging out on one device does not affect other sessions.

### Data ownership

All data you create — categories, datasources, cards, and templates — belongs to you. If you delete your account, your cloud data is removed. Local data stored in your browser is not affected by account deletion and remains accessible until you clear your browser storage.
