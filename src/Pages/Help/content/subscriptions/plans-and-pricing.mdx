## Plans and Pricing

Game Datacards offers a free tier with core features and paid plans for cloud-powered functionality. All local features — datacard rendering, printing, the datasource editor, and local storage — are available for free.

### Subscription tiers

| Tier | Cloud Categories | Cloud Datasources | Templates | Price |
|------|------------------|--------------------|-----------|-------|
| **Free** | 2 | 1 | 2 | Free |
| <IconLabel icon="Crown" label="Premium" color="#faad14" /> | 50 | 2 | 4 | &euro;3.99/month |
| <IconLabel icon="Crown" label="Creator" color="#722ed1" /> | 250 | 10 | 10 | &euro;7.99/month |

### What each tier includes

**Free** — Everything you need to get started. Create and print datacards, build custom datasources, and store everything locally in your browser.

<Icon name="Crown" /> **Premium** — Adds cloud sync, user accounts, and more storage. Sync your categories and datasources across devices. Publish datasources to the community browser.

<Icon name="Crown" /> **Creator** — For power users who manage multiple armies or game systems. Higher limits on cloud categories, datasources, and designer templates. Ideal for content creators and tournament organizers.
