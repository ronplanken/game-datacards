## Getting Started

The Datasource Editor lets you design custom card structures for your tabletop games. A datasource defines everything about your cards — what stats they track, what fields they display, and how the data is organized.

Build datasources for Warhammer 40,000, Age of Sigmar, or any game system you like. Each datasource can hold multiple card types — units, stratagems, enhancements, rules, or whatever your game needs.
