## Card Preview

The center panel shows a live preview of the selected card. How the card looks depends on your base system:

- **Warhammer 40K and Age of Sigmar** cards are rendered in a style that matches the official game format.
- **Custom and Blank** systems use generic card components.

Use the zoom toolbar at the bottom to adjust the view — <Icon name="ZoomIn" /> to zoom in, <Icon name="ZoomOut" /> to zoom out. Toggle **auto-fit** to automatically scale the card to fill the available space, or set a manual zoom level.
