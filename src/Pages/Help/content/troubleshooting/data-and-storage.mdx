## Data and Storage

### Where is my data stored?

All data — categories, cards, datasources, templates, and settings — is stored in your browser's local storage. This means:

- Data is tied to the specific browser and device you are using.
- Clearing your browser data (cache, cookies, site data) will remove everything.
- Private/incognito browsing sessions do not have access to your saved data.

With cloud sync enabled, a copy of your data is also stored on the server. This copy is kept in sync with your local data.

### My data disappeared

If your data has disappeared, check the following:

1. **Browser data cleared** — If you or your browser cleared site data, your local storage was wiped. If you had sync enabled, log in and your cloud data will be restored.
2. **Different browser or profile** — Data is stored per browser profile. Make sure you are using the same browser and profile.
3. **Private browsing** — Private/incognito windows do not share storage with regular windows and are wiped when closed.

### Export as backup

<Callout type="tip">
Export your datasources and templates regularly. These JSON exports are the most reliable backup method and work regardless of browser storage or cloud sync.
</Callout>

### Storage limits

Browser local storage typically has a limit of 5-10 MB per site, depending on the browser. If you have a very large number of cards or templates with embedded images, you may approach this limit. The app does not currently warn you when storage is nearly full.
